ABOUT ME

I graduated with a Bachelors of Science in chemical engineering and took a job in mechanical engineering where I worked independently a majority of the time. Although the job offered a great deal of learning I craved something that gave me a little more creative flexibility and team oriented.

I took a position at a restauraunt and improved my team building and customer service skills. I felt myself in a weird place because I had problem solving and team-building skills. I knew that combining the two could lead to success in another field that I could be passionate about.

Luckily I stumbled upon some free online courses in HTML & CSS and discovered this was something I could pursue as a career. The next step in my learning process was joinging the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp which further extended my knowledge of what I learned independently. By the end of the program I was able to build full stack applications using Node, React, and MongoDB to persist my data. The building blocks I needed were there but I wanted to continue my education in the coding world. It took me a little time to discover what that was until I came across an extremely challenging bootcamp called Zip Code Wimington. The program was particularly in Java but taught me how to use newer technologies such as Java, Angular, and Spring Boot. This program taught me so much that I have the confidence to pursue a career in software development. I feel like I can now successfully implement all of my knowledge I gained throughout my journey.
