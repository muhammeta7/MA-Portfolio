ABOUT ME

I graduated with a degree in chemical engineering and like most, did not work in the field after school. I took a job in mechanical engineering where I learned AutoCad on the fly. In general I field surveyed commercial buildings and addressed the existing HVAC and plumbing issues. There were a lot of situations where I had to implement new products or devices I had never worked with.

Although the job offered a great deal of learning I craved something that gave me a little more creative flexibility. Luckily I stumbled upon some free online courses in HTML & CSS and discovered I loved developing. My next step was to join the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp and it has been the one of the most rewarding life decisions I have ever made. The best part of the program was learning how to implement new and unfamiliar technologies. I am currently learning some languages that were not covered in the program and some side projects.
